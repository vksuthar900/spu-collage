The College is affiliated To J. N. V. University, Jodhpur . The College has around 65
Bigah land area and is situated on Falna-Bali Main Road in Gaurwar Region in Pali District of Rajasthan.
Students from Bali, Mundara, Sadri, Desuri, Ghanerao, Bijapur, Sewari, Bhatoond, Lunawa, Nana, Moribeda,
Pindwara, Sumerpur, Takhatgarh, Sanderao, Rani, Varkana, etc., come here for their studies.

College runs B.Sc.(Maths, Bio), B.A., B.Com., B.C.A., B.B.A., M.Sc. (Computer Science). College have study centers of IGNOU, VMOU & ICSI. It also has the authorized education center of TALLY Training programme.
